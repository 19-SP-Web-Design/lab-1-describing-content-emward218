Always keep in mind that camping spots are hard to find within camping resorts during the summer. Depending on the season, you might want to already be prepared to head out on your camping trip a day before the actual date set.

Always look for other campsites for back up plans. Before you go camping you must prepare by packing your clothes. Pack a pair of clothes for how many days that you will be gone. Do not forget you’ll need shorts for swimming, so pack those. A couple more things to consider is a sweat shirt for the nights that are cold. And a pair of pants for when the mosquitos get bad.
Next you will need to pack equipment for sleeping. Depending how many people that will be paired in a group at night totally depends on your tent size. Pack plastic to lay on the ground in side your tent and a tarp to lay on the ground underneath your tent for when it rains. This will help keep you dry.
Don’t forget you’re cooking equipment, bug spray, lawn chairs, axe, lighter for the fire and so on.
